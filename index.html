Zhicheng Ji Lab, Duke University School of Medicine

About PI

Dr. Zhicheng Ji is a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the Department of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics at Duke University School of Medicine. He received his Ph.D. in Biostatistics and M.S.E. in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University in 2020. His research centers on artificial intelligence and statistical modeling for single-cell and spatial genomics and biomedical imaging, with particular emphasis on large language models, foundation models, and computer vision.
